Aja & Gaucho have been committed Steely Dan fans since birth. Aja's mother even claims to have played Steely Dan for Aja while he was in the womb. These best friends started their band at UMass Amherst as a party joke amongst friends who liked to drink too much tequila, but soon gained a cult following on campus. Now, twenty years later the band still gets together to share their art, talent, and love for Steely Dan to the masses for weddings, barbeques, and any gig that is willing to pay them!
